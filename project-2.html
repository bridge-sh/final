PathFinder

OBJECTIVE

Design an application that fills a specific need or niche. Utilize typography that is appropriate for the app along with a design system. Then develop a prototype of the app using Figma.

I chose to design a job searching app. Pathfinder is an app to assist college students and recent graduates in finding internships or summer jobs. It will also be catered to employers looking for strong applicants to hire. The goal is to make an easy and swift process for applicants to find internships and jobs for a semester at a time.

STYLE GUIDE

Focused on using minimal design elements and colors that are not super saturated and in your face to stay professional. The app is very type heavy and focuses on the information, so I used typefaces that are rounded and have good legibility. Also, the use of organic shapes and graphic elements allows for a more friendly, welcoming tone.
